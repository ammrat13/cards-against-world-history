How to Play

To begin, enter the name of the room you wish to join and a username, then click "Join". In the room, one player will be the Card Czar. Every player except the Card Czar will select the best response (white card) to the prompt (black card) given. The Card Czar will then select the best response out of the ones played, and the player who put down the card will recieve one point. Another player then becomes the Card Czar. Play continues in this manner indefinitely.
